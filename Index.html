About Catering


Tradition since 1889

Over 60 years of Catering Excellence, we have established ourselves as the Leading Vegetarian Caterers in South India. We endorse vegetarianism as we understand the healthy benefits, and the traditional incentive of being a vegetarian. Staunch vegetarians ourselves, we adhere to our strict vegetarianism policy by staffing only authentic Vegetarian Chefs. In addition, all the utensils and cutlery utilized are for cooking and serving Vegetarian Food only. In over 6 decades of catering, we have never catered non-vegetarian food for any event and will continue to excel as Traditional Vegetarian Caterers seasonal ingredients.

We have always led by innovation, be it food presentation, inventing, or improvising our Vegetarian Recipes. We have the distinction of introducing a host of vegetarian delicacies for the first time, which has delighted our customers..
